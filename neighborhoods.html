Enjoy the best of both worlds

Côte d’Azur – The French Riviera is a blissful place where palm trees sway in the warm breeze and the coastline barely misses a day of sun. Here you can still spot the influence of European royalties from long ago and where A-listers have called “home” for decades. Packed with a number of glamorous and chic seaside resorts, the French Riviera is one of the most sophisticated and affluent parts of the world. You can enjoy the best of both worlds by buying a property inland or one of the sought-after half-islands, away from the busy neighbourhoods and tourists, while still being close enough to enjoy the vibrant life in the neighbouring cities when you’re in the mood for it.

There is a place for everyone

The areas around the French Riviera have all something to offer and every place is unique in its feel. Antibes has inspired some of the most talented artists to ever live, while Cannes draws in today’s Hollywood elite every year for the Cannes Film Festival. Set on the hilltop, Èze village has castle ruins and medieval streets filled with galleries and gourmet restaurants, while Menton is influenced by its close proximity to the Italian border. Monaco may not be big on the surface, but its nightlife and variety of dining experiences is huge. Simply put, there is something for everyone on the Cote d’Azur and wherever you choose to put down your luggage on the French Riviera, you’ll find yourself being surrounded by the majestic environment it has to offer.
