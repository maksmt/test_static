Services

Our real estate services on the French Riviera

Marks Real Estate has since the foundation in 2002 offered services and to represent both buyers, sellers, renters and letters on the French Riviera. We proud ourselves with a broad expertise in aiding our clients from the thinking phase to actual implementation of their projects and dreams.

We want to be a long lasting real estate partner to our customers here on the Cote d’Azur. From being able to assist tourists experiencing the country of France, clients looking to acquire a new come home or property owners that want to move on and sell their assets.

In this section you can read about our portfolio of real estate services and get a first introduction to our company. We want to be an active real estate partner that listens to our customers’ needs in order to tailor our services in the most sound way. All from the process of finding the right property investment, to create a plan for your property asset to be let out. Our expertise as real estate agents doesn’t stop when the keys are in your hand, but follow you along the journey when living here and will stand as a sounding board to realize your property dreams.
